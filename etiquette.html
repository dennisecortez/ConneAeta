ConneAeta: Etiquette Essentials

To support the Nabuclod Aeta community in maintaining their traditions and privacy, we have outlined essential etiquette protocols. These guidelines are divided into two categories: the Do’s, which highlight how you can actively show respect and appreciation for the culture; and the Don’ts, which identify the boundaries necessary to safeguard Aeta heritage and rights. We ask that you honor these protocols to ensure our connection remains one of integrity and shared humanity.
